Saving Food, Saving The World

发表于 2021-03-20 分类于 文学创作 ， 英文作品 ， 演讲稿
本文字数： 1.8k 阅读时长 ≈ 2 分钟

Every morning, after having breakfast in the canteen, I have not found how to find the empty trash can has been filled with leftovers, until one day.

That afternoon, I was too late to queue for the dinner in the hope of playing badminton after my PE class, only to find that there was no food waiting in the steaming for the hungry student.
I had to take a plate of vegetables and a bowl of rice in frustration, head down back to his seat, silently gulping cold and tasteless food. Smelling the scent left over from the deserted canteen, I finally tasted what it was like to be hungry. And when I finally gave up chewing the little vegetables and took the plate to throw them away, I was shocked by the full trash can. That’s a lot of meat, vegetables and rice! It could be the only weapon to solve hunger for me, but was so cruelly abandoned in the swill bucket despised by the public. I began to feel sorry and sad, looking at the rice soup in the swill bucket, and then looking at the green vegetables curled up in my hands, I silently walked back to my seat, and finished the rest of my dinner in silence.
Then, for the first time, I realized what waste was.
I started to focus on the issue of food waste, and found that the number of food waste in human beings is staggering! According to the United Nations, 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted every year - that’s about a third of the world’s total food. In China alone, the restaurants wasted 17 to 18 million tons of food a year from 2013 to 2015. That could feed 30 to 50 million people for a whole year, according to Chinese Academy of Sciences.
At the same time, in 2020, there will still be 690 million people in the world living in a state of perennial hunger, which is equivalent to one out of every 10 people going without food!
Now, please imagine if we pooled all the wasted food, would there still be hunger in the world? But it is precisely because of the contemporary people’s pursuit of gold and profit, extravagance and waste of the atmosphere is too prevalent, there is food waste everywhere, so in some poor areas, there will be people without food, perennial hunger.
Finally, let’s cherish food, save food and eliminate waste. Starts from us, saving food, saving the world!
